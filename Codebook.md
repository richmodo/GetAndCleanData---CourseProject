# Codebook
The original data set is taken from the following location:
http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones

The data has been 'tidied' as per the Coursera Course, Get and Clean Data, ProjectRrequirements.  The output of 'tidydata' is listed below. There are 79 'feature' variables for Mean and Standard Deviation.   


##Identifiers
'subject' refers to identity (1-30) of each volunteer
'activity' refers to the measurement activity that the subject was performing (WALKING, WALKING UPSTAIRS, WALKNG DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)

## Measurement variables (also refered to as 'feature' variables).
The subjects wore a Samsung Smartphone smartphone embedded with a Gyroscope and Accelerometer sensors whilst performing the activities.
